# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Retail investors and active traders who want a personal finance OS for tracking crypto and stock markets, managing a portfolio, and monitoring watchlists and transactions. They use it on desktop at home and on mobile while checking markets on the go. They expect professional-grade clarity — not a toy, not a bank.

## Product Purpose

Meridian is a unified market intelligence and portfolio management tool. It surfaces crypto and equity data in one place, lets users build watchlists, track their portfolio value over time, and review transaction history. Success looks like: a user opens the app, reads the market at a glance, and has full confidence in their position.

## Brand Personality

Sleek, precise, powerful — Bloomberg meets modern fintech. The interface should feel like a tool built for someone who takes markets seriously, not a game or a social app. Confidence through restraint: data speaks first, decoration second.

## Anti-references

- Robinhood: playful rounded shapes, confetti, gamification signals
- Generic crypto apps: neon gradients, aggressive green/red theming, loud price tickers
- TradingView (overcrowded): information density as a value in itself
- Consumer bank apps: overly soft, pastel, hand-holdy

## Design Principles

1. **Data speaks first.** Every visual decision should direct attention toward the numbers and signals, not away from them. Chrome earns its place or it disappears.
2. **Precision over decoration.** Pixel-accurate alignment, consistent sizing, intentional whitespace. Nothing is approximate.
3. **Professional by default.** The app should feel like a tool a serious investor would trust. Not institutional-cold, but not playful either — expert-calm.
4. **Responsive without regression.** Mobile is not a degraded desktop. The layout adapts to give the same signal fidelity at any screen size.
5. **Dark native.** The dark theme is the primary visual register — not a toggle, not an option. It is the product's identity.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA minimum. Reduced-motion alternative for all animations. High contrast ratios on dark backgrounds (text ≥ 4.5:1, UI components ≥ 3:1). Keyboard navigation across all interactive elements.
